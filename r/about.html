QRoll

QRoll is an experiment to study how people's curiosity can prompt them to scan a QR code without any context, and obtain statistics based on the geographic location of that QR code associated with a unique 3-digit code, the first of the three digits is used to identify the collocator of that QR code while the last two are used as a unique identification code.
